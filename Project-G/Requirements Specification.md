## 2. Scope

### 2.1 Capabilities
- **Isomorphic Mapping:** Physical implementation of logical problems as potential wells (attractors) in phase space.
- **Autonomous Relaxation:** State transition driven solely by the system's Lyapunov property ($\dot{J} \leq 0$).

- **Atemporal Extraction:** Operational focus strictly on "Initial Injection" and "Final Extraction," bypassing intermediate steps.

### 2.2 Constraints
- **Dynamic Causal Control:** No interruption of logical gates during relaxation (no rewriting of causal sequences).
- **Reversible Computation:** Tracking the computational process backward is impossible due to reliance on entropy-increasing physical relaxation.
