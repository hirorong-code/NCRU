## A Geometry-Free Core of PMNT

structures. Computation is defined as autonomous energy minimization
governed by gradient flow. A quartic scalar potential with a
control-dependent quadratic coefficient is sufficient to implement state
selection and structural transitions corresponding to computational
branching.
